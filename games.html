Terraria

Terraria is a 2D sandbox game that can go either singleplayer or multiplayer. This game revolves around exploration, building, mining, combat and others.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite

  • You can do alot of things in this game.
  • It has many bosses that are challenging.
  • You can improve your experience in this game using mods.
  • You have to grind for things instead of getting it easily.
  • You can play this with friends.
